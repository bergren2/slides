EASY

It's just:
  • HTML
  • CSS

Your presentation doesn't need to be fancy, it just needs to be consistent so that it isn't jarring.

Not jarring in a bad way, anyway.
Notice how my fonts and spacing are consistent! And Big takes care of styling for italics, bold, underline, and links.

EASY AND FUN
Do:
  • Pick a topic relevant to the team and your interests.
  • Run through your presentation ahead of time.
  • Get it on the calendar so you have a deadline to work towards.
Don't:
  • Present longer than you need to.
  • Use Big if you're more comfortable using PowerPoint.
  • Demo if you don't want to.
Honestly, your presentation could just be you reading slides. As long as the slides aren't boring.
Unless your name is Elon Musk.
